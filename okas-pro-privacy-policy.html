1. Introduction

OKAS Pro – Smart Home Control (“App”) is a smart home automation and control application.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

By accessing or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

________________________________________

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide smart home automation, user management, and communication features.

________________________________________

2.1 Login & Authentication Information

To access the App, users must verify their identity using:

  • Phone Number
  • Email Address

An OTP (One-Time Password) is sent for authentication and security purposes.

This information is used strictly for:

  • Login authentication
  • Account security
________________________________________

2.2 Member & Guest Profile Information

The Admin user can create additional users (Member or Guest). The following information may be collected:

  1. Profile Photo
  2. Full Name
  3. Nick Name
  4. Member Type
  5. Phone Number
  6. Date of Birth
  7. Gender
  8. Email Address
  9. Language Preference

This information is used for:

  • User identification within the household
  • Role-based access control
  • Permission management
  • Personalization of user experience
________________________________________

2.3 Device & Permission Data

The App allows the Admin to assign access permissions to:

  • Rooms within the property
  • Smart appliances
  • Media systems
  • Automation controls and scenes

We may store:

  • Device configuration settings
  • User-to-device permission mapping
  • Operational logs required for system functionality
________________________________________

2.4 Communication & SIP Features

The App includes SIP-based voice and/or video communication functionality for supported SIP-based door phone systems.

For communication features:

  • SIP credentials may be configured
  • Audio/video data is transmitted in real-time through configured SIP servers
  • We do not record or store call audio or video

Communication data is used only to provide real-time calling functionality.

________________________________________

2.5 Permissions Used by the App

The App may request the following permissions to function properly:

  • Camera – Used for video communication and profile photo capture.
  • Microphone – Used during voice or video communication.
  • Internet & Network Access – Required to connect to smart devices and SIP servers.
  • Notifications – Used to display alerts such as incoming calls or device updates.
  • Foreground Service – Used to maintain active communication sessions.
  • Boot Completed – Used to restore essential services after device restart.
  • Media/Storage Access – Used to select profile images.

These permissions are requested only when required and are used strictly for App functionality. The App does not access device resources for background monitoring or advertising purposes.
